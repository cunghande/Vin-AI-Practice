# Luat Phong, chong ma tuy 2021

Luat Phong, chong ma tuy 2021 quy dinh trach nhiem phong ngua, dau tranh, kiem soat va cai nghien ma tuy. Van ban nhan manh moi ca nhan, gia dinh, co quan, to chuc va cong dong co trach nhiem tham gia phong chong ma tuy.

Nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy co the bi quan ly theo quy dinh phap luat. Cac bien phap cai nghien, ho tro sau cai nghien va hoa nhap cong dong can thuc hien theo dung thu tuc, bao dam quyen loi hop phap cua nguoi lien quan.
